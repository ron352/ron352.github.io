I am Sarmistha Sarna - currently serving as a lecturer in Software Engineering deparment at Daffodil International University and continuing my M.Sc at the department of Computer Science and Engineering in Jahangirnagar University (JU) under the supervision of Professor Mohammad Shorif Uddin. My research interests are spread across Human Computer Interaction , Data Mining, Federated Learning, Usable security and privacy, Applied AI to integrate qualitative and quantitative approaches for developing user-friendly solutions to both societal and technological problems. Besides human psychology, I am also intrigued about psycholinguistics, and policymaking. I'm inquisitive in developing novel techniques to facilitate efficient, explainable, robust frameworks for end users, keeping mind in fairness and biasness.

My hair color changes a lot so my profile picture ain't an accurate depiction of me. Apart from this, I'm into mountains, solo-backpacking, cycling and exploring music genres (specially Gazal and 80's rock songs).
